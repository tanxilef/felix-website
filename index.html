About

Curious first, polished second.

I grew up wanting to be a travel journalist, inspired by stories that made faraway places feel alive. I eventually took the science and engineering route, but the storytelling itch never really left.

These days I split my time between research and creating — documenting unconventional destinations, overland routes, language attempts, awkward moments, kind strangers, and practical lessons from the road.

I'm Indonesian-born, Singapore-based, and especially drawn to places people misunderstand or overlook. My content is for people who want to travel beyond the obvious, without pretending money, time, or confidence are unlimited.
